About Us

At Cottontail Childcare & Education, our mission is to foster a supportive, home-like environment that encourages children to explore, learn, and develop essential life skills. We believe in individualized attention, achieved by maintaining a small group size and the dedicated care of our owner and teacher, who is deeply passionate about early childhood education.

Whether you’re searching for a caring daycare in Springboro or a preschool program near Dayton, we welcome children from all backgrounds to join our family. Come visit us to see how our nurturing atmosphere can help your little one thrive!
